A different kind of product development agency

We have a fundamental responsibility as arbiters of the experiences we create to not think like marketers, or salesmen, or even customers - but as people, who are looking for an answer, a little joy, or something different.

It is our job at Noctivity to start every project with a clean white piece of paper and create something both wanted and slightly magical.

Launched Oct, 2019 for Apeel Sciences. FruitStand.com lets people discover, experience and try new and exotic fruit direct from the farm. The Noctivity team created FruitStand to help Apeel meet their goals of empowering local farms while building a community of consumers interested in specialty produce. Noctivity worked with Apeel to develop the product packaging, inserts, fulfillment, customer management, web experience, social media strategy and campaign and eCommerce store.

The Results
  • Ideation to launch within six weeks
  • 6X return on ad spend (ROAS)
  • Per order profitability within 10 weeks
  • Product sold out two months in advance

2017 - 2019 for PingGPS. PingGPS offered the smallest and longest lasting GPS tracking device on the market. Noctivity worked with Ping to develop the marketing, brand, packaging and customer experience that was instrumental in becoming the second most successful crowd-funded tracking device ever on IndieGoGo at the time.

The Results
  • Over 1.25M raised in pre-orders within 60 days
  • Second most successful tracking product crowdfunded
  • 8X return on ad spend (ROAS)

2008 - 2018 for DMV Study Guides Inc. DMV Study Guides is one of the largest providers of study material to help people pass their DMV written test. Since 2008, Noctivity has been responsible for the strategy, design, technical implementatio, partnership development, marketing and conversion rate optimization behind DMVCheatSheets.com, the flagship offering.

The Results
  • Largest provider of DMV study guides on the Internet
  • Over 12M customers
  • Position marketing ROI on all channels
  • Partnership network developed for distribution
